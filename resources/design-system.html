Clean White Gallery

Design system for neilhurley.com — a photography portfolio built on warm whites, refined typography, and generous space that lets the work breathe.

Colours

A warm, neutral palette anchored by off-white backgrounds and a muted bronze accent. The palette avoids pure white (#fff) in favour of warmer tones that complement photography without competing with it.

Typography

Libre Baskerville for display and headings — a refined transitional serif that reads as editorial and trustworthy. Outfit for body, navigation and UI — a geometric sans with excellent legibility at light weights.
